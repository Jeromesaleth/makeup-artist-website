Keratin Treatments

We understand the importance of having smooth, frizz-free hair. Our Keratin Treatments are designed to transform your hair into a sleek, shiny masterpiece. These treatments are perfect for those seeking to reduce frizz, improve manageability, and enhance the overall health of their hair.

Keratin is a natural protein found in hair, skin, and nails. A keratin treatment involves applying a keratin-rich solution to the hair, which is then sealed in with heat. This process smooths the hair cuticle, resulting in a polished, glossy finish.

Balayage

we specialize in the art of Balayage, a freehand hair colouring technique that creates a beautiful, natural-looking gradient. Balayage is perfect for those who want to add dimension and brightness to their hair without the commitment of traditional highlights.

Balayage is a French term meaning "to sweep." This technique involves hand-painting highlights onto the hair, starting at the mid-lengths and gradually lightening towards the ends. The result is a sun-kissed effect that looks soft and blended, mimicking the natural highlights created by the sun.
